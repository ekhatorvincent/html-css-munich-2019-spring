stew and white rice as we fondly call it in Nigeria. I love making my own stew but I also enjoy tasting stew made
by other people, it’s always amazing how stew can
have different unique flavors and tastes when prepared by different people even
with the same ingredients. Stew is very versatile as it can be served with different
types of sides such as steamed rice, boiled or fried yam, plantains, potatoes, egusi soup, okra soup and even bread…Yes,
I love stew and fresh ‘out of the oven bread’
